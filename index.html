Why It Matters

More Than Just Words on a Page

Literacy is essential for health, employment, and navigating modern society. Yet, millions of adults and children lack basic reading skills.

At "Literacy is Key," we break the cycle of poverty by empowering individuals with the tools to read, write, and succeed.
